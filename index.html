Getting Started with IsoLayer v2.4.0

Step 1 of 3 — Sandbox Setup

IsoLayer requires a containerized sandbox with access to the host's recovery partition for its snapshot engine.

Start a Docker container. Mount the host's /root to /mnt/recovery.
