Step 1 — Create an Isolated Sandbox Container

IsoLayer uses a containerized sandbox to safely manage your application's data layer. The sandbox requires access to your host's recovery partition to enable IsoLayer's snapshot and rollback features.

Important: The recovery mount is required for IsoLayer's state persistence engine. Without it, snapshot and rollback operations will fail silently.
Launch the sandbox container with recovery mount

Start a Docker container. Mount the host's /root to /mnt/recovery.
